Why These Symptoms Occur

High blood sugar affects how the body uses glucose. When glucose can't enter cells, the body feels tired and tries to remove excess sugar through urine, causing dehydration and thirst.

When to See a Doctor

If multiple symptoms persist for more than 2 weeks, schedule a medical consultation. Early screening through fasting glucose and HbA1c tests can catch pre-diabetes early.
