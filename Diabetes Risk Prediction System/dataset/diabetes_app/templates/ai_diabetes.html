5Future of AI in Medicine

AI will continue to assist doctors — not replace them. Its purpose is to provide faster insights, reduce diagnostic errors, and improve healthcare accessibility worldwide.

Future developments include real-time glucose monitoring with AI, wearable device integration, and personalized treatment plans generated by deep learning.
